Our Story

Fusion Dev Consulting was founded on a simple belief: complex projects don't need complex organizations. We started with deep expertise in Microsoft technologies and a passion for helping companies modernize and automate their operations using AI and intelligent automation.

Today, we work with organizations across finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and public sector to deploy cutting-edge solutions that drive real business impact. Our lean team model means direct access to senior consultants — no junior staff, no committees, just results.

We call ourselves "Fusion" because we fuse different technologies, methodologies, and ideas into cohesive solutions that actually work.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2010

Beginning of the Journey

Both founders began specializing in Microsoft technologies at leading consulting firms, building expertise in SharePoint, .Net, Power Platform, and integration solutions.

2019

Power Platform Evolution

As Power Platform matured, we recognized the opportunity to help enterprises automate complex processes. Started building deep expertise in Power Automate and RPA migration.

2022

AI & Agents Focus

With AI emerging as a transformative force, we pivoted to specialize in intelligent agents, Copilot development, and OpenAI integration alongside traditional automation.

2024

Fusion Dev Founded

Two consultants with 30+ combined years decided to go independent. Founded Fusion Dev to deliver expert, direct consulting without unnecessary overhead.
