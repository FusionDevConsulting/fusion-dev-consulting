FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can you start on a project?

Typically within 1–2 weeks of contract signature for standard engagements. For urgent projects, we can sometimes fast-track if our schedule permits.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. We're fully remote and have worked with clients across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Asia. We adapt to your timezone.

What engagement models do you offer?

We offer fixed-scope project delivery, time-and-materials, and monthly retainer arrangements. We'll recommend the best fit based on your project type.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes. We offer post-launch support retainers, quarterly health checks, and ad-hoc support arrangements to keep your solutions running smoothly.

Can you assess our existing automation estate?

Yes — we offer automation discovery workshops where we assess your current bots, flows, and processes and provide a prioritised roadmap with ROI estimates.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're comfortable signing NDAs before any initial discussions. We understand the sensitivity of business process and technology information.
